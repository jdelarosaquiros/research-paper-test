\section*{Limitations}

GSM8k is the one dataset where the standalone consistency estimator regresses ($-$2.2\%), likely due to its small training set interacting with the increased model capacity.
The combined estimators still show gains on GSM8k, suggesting that the multiplicative combination with confidence signals compensates for variance in the learned consistency score.
Our experiments are limited to a single model (Mistral-7B) and decoding strategy (greedy); evaluation across additional models and sampling strategies is needed to assess generality.
