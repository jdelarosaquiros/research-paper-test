\citet{cocoa2024}, reporting PRR$_{0.5}$ (normalized) under greedy decoding across six
datasets. Generation metrics follow the paper: AlignScore\textsubscript{Input$\to$Output} for
XSUM, COMET for WMT19\,De$\to$En, AlignScore\textsubscript{Output$\to$Target} for CoQA, and
Accuracy for TriviaQA, MMLU, and GSM8k. WMT14\,Fr$\to$En is omitted (no results available).
